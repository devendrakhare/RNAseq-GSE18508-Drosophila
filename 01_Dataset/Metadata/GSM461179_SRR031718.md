## Sample Description

This sample represents Drosophila S2-DRSC cells following RNA interference (RNAi) targeting the **CG8144** gene. It is the treated sample used to investigate transcriptional changes associated with CG8144 knockdown.

## Role in Analysis

This is the RNAi-treated sample used for differential gene expression analysis. Gene expression from this sample was compared with three untreated control samples to identify genes whose expression changed following CG8144 knockdown.
