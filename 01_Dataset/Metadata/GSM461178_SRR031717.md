## Sample Description

This sample is the third untreated biological replicate from the Drosophila S2-DRSC cell line. Together with the other control samples, it improves the statistical reliability of downstream differential expression analysis.

## Role in Analysis

This sample serves as an untreated control and provides the baseline gene expression profile for comparison with the CG8144 RNAi-treated sample during differential expression analysis.
